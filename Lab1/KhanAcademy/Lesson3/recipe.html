Pancakes

  • Time: 20 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredient Quantity
Flour 1 cup
Milk 1 cup
Egg 1 large
Sugar 2 tbsp

Steps:

  1. Mix flour, sugar, milk, and egg in a bowl until smooth.
  2. Heat a lightly oiled skillet over medium heat.
  3. Pour batter onto the skillet and cook until bubbles form.
  4. Flip and cook until golden brown on both sides.

Spaghetti Carbonara

  • Time: 30 minutes
  • Serves: 2
Ingredient Quantity
Spaghetti 200g
Pancetta 100g
Eggs 2 large
Parmesan Cheese 50g

Steps:

  1. Cook spaghetti in boiling water until al dente.
  2. Fry pancetta in a pan until crispy.
  3. Mix eggs and Parmesan in a bowl.
  4. Drain spaghetti and combine with pancetta, then mix in the egg mixture.

Chocolate Cake

  • Time: 1 hour
  • Serves: 8
Ingredient Quantity
Flour 1 3/4 cups
Sugar 2 cups
Cocoa Powder 3/4 cup
Butter 1 cup

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Mix flour, sugar, and cocoa powder in a bowl.
  3. Add melted butter and mix until smooth.
  4. Pour into a greased pan and bake for 35 minutes.
