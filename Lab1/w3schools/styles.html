The game is a loose adaptation of Peter Jackson's Lord of the Rings film trilogy: The
 Fellowship of the Ring (2001), The Two Towers (2002) and The Return of the King (2003). As it
 is not an adaptation of J. R. R. Tolkien's 1954 novel The Lord of the Rings, anything
 from the novels not specifically mentioned or depicted in the films could not be represented in the game.
